### 1. State of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Research at ICLR 2026
- **作者**: Moritz Reuss
- **年份**: 2025 (October)
- **链接**: https://mbreuss.github.io/blog_post_iclr_26_vla.html
- **摘要**: Comprehensive analysis of 164 Vision-Language-Action (VLA) model submissions at ICLR 2026. Discover trends in discrete diffusion VLAs, reasoning models, benchmarks (LIBERO, CALVIN, SIMPLER), and the gap between frontier and academic research.

### 2. Embodied Robot Manipulation in the Era of Foundation Models: Planning and Learning Perspectives
- **作者**: arXiv:2512.22983
- **年份**: 2025 (December)
- **链接**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.22983

### 3. A Comprehensive Survey on World Models for Embodied AI
- **作者**: arXiv:2510.16732
